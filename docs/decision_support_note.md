# What Decisions This Copilot Is Meant to Support

## Purpose

This note defines the business questions the analytics copilot answers
and the decisions each output is designed to support. Every dashboard
view, generated narrative, and anomaly flag maps back to one of these.

---

## Business Questions and Decision Support

### 1. Where is revenue growing or declining?
**Decision it supports:** Budget allocation, category investment, and
promotional planning.
**Intended audience:** Executive, Finance.

---

### 2. Which sellers are underperforming or creating customer risk?
**Decision it supports:** Seller intervention, suspension, or coaching.
**Intended audience:** Operations, Vendor Management.

---

### 3. Are delivery times getting worse, and where?
**Decision it supports:** Carrier selection, seller shipping requirements,
regional logistics investment.
**Intended audience:** Operations, Logistics.

---

### 4. Which product categories drive the most value?
**Decision it supports:** Assortment strategy, category expansion,
marketing spend allocation.
**Intended audience:** Executive, Category Management.

---

### 5. Are there anomalies in order volume, revenue, or seller quality
that require immediate attention?
**Decision it supports:** Escalation decisions, operational alerts,
and root cause investigations.
**Intended audience:** Analyst, Operations.

---

### 6. What is the repeat purchase rate and which segments retain best?
**Decision it supports:** Customer retention strategy, loyalty program
design, CRM targeting.
**Intended audience:** Marketing, Executive.

---

### 7. What changed this week or month, why does it matter, and what
should be investigated next?
**Decision it supports:** Weekly business review preparation, executive
briefing, and analyst prioritization.
**Intended audience:** Executive, Analyst.

---

## Output Types by Audience

| Audience | Output Type | Format |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| Executive | KPI summary, narrative brief | PDF memo, dashboard summary tab |
| Analyst | Drill-down views, anomaly flags, raw tables | Dashboard, CSV exports |
| Operations | Seller defect flags, delivery alerts | Dashboard alerts tab |
